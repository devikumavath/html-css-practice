maggi recipe

  1. put the vessel on stove
  2. pour 1 and half cup of water
  3. add maggi masala
  4. break the maggi cake and add in the vessel
  5. add some veggies like :
    1. carrot
    2. peas
    3. onion
  6. cook for 2 mins
  7. yummy yummy maggi is ready...
